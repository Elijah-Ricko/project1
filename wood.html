Types of Wood

Types of Wood Pine Oak Poplar Spruce Cherry Maple plywood mdf pegboard Yellow tone vs Red tone; Hardwood vs Softwood; Good for stain vs Bad for stain (more on stain page)

There are a lot of decisions that play into which species of wood you should choose for a project. You need to think about Hardwood vs Softwood, warm tone vs cool tone, and if it's to be stained or painted. These decisions all boil down to what will look and feel the best for this project. While there are some characteristics that can help guide your decision, it comes down to personal preference. Below, there will be a breakdown of those characteristics to assist the decision making.
